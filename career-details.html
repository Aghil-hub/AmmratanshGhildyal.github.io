Senior Manager, Business Intelligence Unit - Digital Business & Transformation

Oct 2022 - Jun 2025

Senior Consultant, Business Consulting

  • Financial services analytics consulting

Mu Sigma

Decision Scientist

Aug 2017 - Nov 2020

Mu Sigma was where I built my foundation in decision science, working on complex operational and analytics problems for Fortune 500 clients across insurance and retail. This stage shaped the way I approach analytics even today: start with the decision context, understand the business process, and use data not just to explain what happened, but to guide what should happen next.

  • Decision science for Fortune 500 clients
  • Insurance and retail analytics exposure
  • Operational and business problem solving
  • Strong analytical fundamentals
